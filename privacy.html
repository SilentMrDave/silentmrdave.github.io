Silent Spire Studios Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines the data practices for Silent Spire Studios apps on the Google Play Store. Currently, this includes Human Invasion and Nugget Wing.

Information Collection

Silent Spire Studios does not directly collect any personal information from users of our apps.

Third-Party Advertising

Our apps include advertising from AdMob (a Google service). AdMob may collect the following information to deliver more relevant ads:

  • Device identifiers
  • General location data (not precise GPS data)
  • Information about your interactions with ads

How AdMob May Use Information

AdMob may use this information to:

  • Deliver ads that are more relevant to your interests
  • Track the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns
  • Limit the number of times you see the same ad

Changes to this Policy

We may update this privacy policy in the future. Any changes will be posted here.
